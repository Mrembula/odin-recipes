Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among Muslim of South Asia. It is made with Indian spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat lamb, prawn and fish) or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, egg and potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1(2 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks
  • 1 pound basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 5 pods cardamom
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 pinch powdered saffron

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet. Fry potatoes in hot oil until lightly browned, about 3 to 5 minutes. Remove to a paper towel-lined plate tp drain; set aside
  2. Add remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet. Add onions, garlic and fresh ginger; cook and stir until onion is soft and golden.Add tomatoes, salt, cumin, chill powder, pepper and turmeric; cook, stirring constantly, for 5 minute. Stir in in yogurt, mint, ground cardamom, and cinnamon stick. Cover and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes are cooked to a pulp. It may be necessary to add a little hot water if mixture becomes too dry and starts to stick to the pan
  3. Add chicken and stir well to coat. Cover and cook over vry low heat until chicken is tender, 35 to 45 minutes. There should only be a little very thick gravy left when chicken is finished cooking. If necessary cook uncovered for few minutes to reduce the gravy
  4. Meanwhile, make the rice:Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes
  5. Heat oil in a larger skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until golden. Add cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, ground ginger and saffron; stir in rice until coates with spices
  6. Heat stock and salt in a medium pot until hot; poyr over rice and stir well. Add chicken mixture and potatoes; stir gently to combing.Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to very low, cover with a tight-fitting lid, and steam for 20 minutes without the lid or stirring. Spoon biryani onto a warm serving dish
